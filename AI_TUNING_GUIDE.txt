================================================================================
                    OpenTrickler AI Tuning Guide
================================================================================

WHAT IS AI TUNING?
------------------
AI Tuning automatically finds the best motor speed settings for your trickler.
Instead of manually adjusting values, the trickler tests different settings
and learns what works best for your powder and setup.


BEFORE YOU START
----------------
1. Make sure your scale is connected and zeroed
2. Have powder in the hopper
3. Place an empty pan on the scale
4. Select the profile you want to tune


HOW TO USE AI TUNING
--------------------
1. Open the OpenTrickler web portal in your browser
2. Go to Settings > AI Tuning
3. Select your profile (0-7)
4. Enter your target charge weight (e.g., 42.5 grains)
5. Click "Start"
6. Wait while the trickler runs test drops (up to 30 drops)
7. When done, click "Apply Recommended Parameters" to save


WHAT YOU'LL SEE DURING TUNING
-----------------------------
The Status badge will show:

  "phase1_coarse" = Tuning the coarse (fast) motor
  "phase2_fine"   = Tuning the fine (slow) motor

The drop counter shows how many test drops have been completed.
A log shows each drop's results and what settings will be tried next.


WHEN IT'S DONE
--------------
The Status badge turns green and shows "complete".
Recommended settings appear on screen.
Click "Apply Recommended Parameters" to save them to your profile.
Click "Cancel" if you want to keep your old settings.


MACHINE LEARNING
----------------
Every time you throw a charge, the trickler remembers what happened.
After a few charges, it may suggest small improvements to your settings.
You can apply these suggestions from the web portal.


TIPS
----
- Don't bump the scale during tuning
- Use the same powder you'll be loading with
- If tuning fails, try adjusting the parameter ranges in settings
- Tuning typically takes 10-20 drops to complete


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
"Error: Did not converge"
  - The trickler couldn't find good settings in 30 drops
  - Try widening the parameter ranges in settings
  - Check that your scale is stable

Tuning seems slow?
  - This is normal - the trickler is being careful to find optimal settings
  - Each drop teaches it something new

Settings don't feel right after tuning?
  - Click "Cancel" to restore your previous settings
  - Try tuning again with a different target weight
